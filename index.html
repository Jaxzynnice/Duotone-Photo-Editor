Frequently Asked Questions

Duotone Photo Editor is a free online tool that allows you to apply beautiful duotone effects to your photos. You can choose between Brave Pink, Hero Green, or a Combined effect, with options to customize intensity and color settings.

Yes! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

We support JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, ICO, HEIC, GIF, BMP, and TIFF formats. There is no file size limit since all processing happens in your browser.

After applying your desired effects, click the 'Download' button to save the image to your device. You can choose from multiple formats including PNG, JPEG, WebP, and more. You can also use the 'Share' button to share it directly to social media platforms.

Yes! Duotone Photo Editor is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. You can even swipe on the preview image to switch between effects.

You can undo up to 3 previous steps. This allows you to experiment with different settings while maintaining the ability to go back to recent changes.
